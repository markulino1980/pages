Bats BZX Exchange, Inc. (BatsBZX) Rulemaking

The BATS Exchange has changed its name to Bats BZX Exchange, Inc.. All filings by Bats BZX Exchange will be posted to the Bats BZX page. Filings filed with "BATS" as part of the file number will be posted on the BATS Exchange page.

We encourage the public to submit comments on the following Bats BZX Exchange, Inc. (BatsBZX) filings during the comment period. For detailed instructions, please read How to Submit Comments. We strongly encourage you to send your comments electronically. They will be posted on this website.
